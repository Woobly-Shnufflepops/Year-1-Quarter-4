Grouping Tables Coloumns

The <colgroup> tag specifies a group of one or more columns in a table for styling.
The <colgroup> tag must be a child of <table> tag.
It should come after any <cpation> tagbut before thead, tfoot, or tbody.

Example:

Cell 5 Cell 6 Cell 7 Cell 8

Cell 13 Cell 14 Cell 15 Cell 16
